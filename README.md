# Consciousness bibliography (Chalmers “Facing”)

Local copies of open-access texts from the **References** and **Further Reading** sections of David Chalmers’s [*Facing Up to the Problem of Consciousness*](https://consc.net/papers/facing.html), including that article and his follow-up reply as files from [consc.net](https://consc.net/).

- **`Chalmers-1995-Facing-Up-to-the-Problem-of-Consciousness.pdf`** — “Facing Up…” (PDF from [consc.net/papers/facing.pdf](https://consc.net/papers/facing.pdf)).
- **`Chalmers-1997-Moving-Forward-on-the-Problem-of-Consciousness.html`** — “Moving Forward…” (HTML from [consc.net/papers/moving.html](https://consc.net/papers/moving.html); no PDF is linked there).

**`SOURCES.txt`** lists each file, its bibliographic citation, and where it was downloaded. Items that could not be obtained (books, paywalled papers, etc.) are noted at the bottom of that file.
