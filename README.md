# Consciousness bibliography (Chalmers “Facing”)

Local copies of open-access texts from the **References** and **Further Reading** sections of David Chalmers’s [*Facing Up to the Problem of Consciousness*](https://consc.net/papers/facing.html), plus the article itself in PDF and HTML.

- **`Chalmers-1995-Facing-Up-to-the-Problem-of-Consciousness.pdf`** — journal version (PDF from [consc.net](https://consc.net/papers/facing.pdf)).
- **`facing.html`** — HTML version of the same article from the author’s site.

**`SOURCES.txt`** lists each file, its bibliographic citation, and where it was downloaded. Items that could not be obtained (books, paywalled papers, etc.) are noted at the bottom of that file.

Texts are stored for personal research and study. Copyright remains with authors and publishers; remove or replace files if your use is not permitted.
